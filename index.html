About Me

Takeo was born and raised in the East Bay Area. Growing up, he played baseball, track, cross country, and basketball. He still enjoys being active, and enjoyed hiking and traveling.

After graduating from High School, Takeo attended community college, and then transferred to UC Berkeley where he studied Business Administration.

Takeo currently works at a tech startup, and dreams of starting his own company that will revolutionize the world of work.
